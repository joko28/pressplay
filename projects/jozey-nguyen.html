The Wolf’s Journey: Walking Through Dreamscapes

Artist Statement

The Wolf’s Journey: Walking Through Dreamscapes is a piece where I get to step out of reality and see life through the eyes of my spirit animal, the wolf. In this project, I imagine myself walking through surreal, hand-drawn landscapes that let me explore emotions and thoughts I don’t always share in my day-to-day life.
Each scene represents a different part of my inner world—from peaceful moments to chaotic ones.
