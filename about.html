About the Exhibition

“Press Play” is a digital media exhibition that explores the interactive and immersive nature of art in the digital age. The exhibition invites audiences to engage with the works presented, encouraging them to become active participants in the artistic experience.

Concept

At the heart of “Press Play” is the idea that art should be a participatory experience. By blending various digital mediums, the exhibition aims to challenge traditional boundaries between creator and viewer, inviting audiences to play, explore, and interact with the artworks in innovative ways.

Artistic Vision

This exhibition showcases a diverse range of works, including interactive installations, video art, and 3-dimensional. Each piece is designed to provoke thought, inspire creativity, and foster a sense of community among participants. “Press Play” is not just an exhibition; it is an invitation to enter a new realm of artistic expression and exploration.
