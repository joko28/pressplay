About the Exhibition

This exhibition showcases the most amazing retro-inspired pixel art, blending nostalgia with modern-day creativity. The art pieces range from vibrant landscapes to intricate character designs, each bringing the charm of pixel art to life.

Concept

At the heart of “Press Play” is the idea that art should be a participatory experience. By blending various digital mediums, the exhibition aims to challenge traditional boundaries between creator and viewer, inviting audiences to play, explore, and interact with the artworks in innovative ways.


Artistic Vision

This exhibition showcases a diverse range of works, including interactive installations, video art, and 3-dimensional pieces. Each piece is designed to provoke thought, inspire creativity, and foster a sense of community among participants. “Press Play” is not just an exhibition; it is an invitation to enter a new realm of artistic expression and exploration.


Join Us

We encourage you to engage with the art and experience it in new, interactive ways.
